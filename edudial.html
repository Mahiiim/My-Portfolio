About Project

EduDial is an IVR-based education platform designed to reduce the digital divide in rural Bangladesh. Students can access educational lessons through simple phone calls and SMS without needing smartphones or internet access. The system uses BDApps Voice API and SMS API to deliver accessible learning solutions.
