Achievements

Awards and Project Recognition

EduDial

Top 25 — BDApps Innovation Summit 2025

EduDial is an innovative IVR-based education platform developed to make learning accessible for students without smartphones or internet access. The project was recognized as one of the Top 25 teams among more than 1500 participants at the BDApps Innovation Summit 2025. The system demonstrates the practical use of telecommunication technology to reduce the digital divide and improve educational accessibility in rural areas.

AI-Powered Interactive Robot

Top 5 — Annual Project Fair of ETE, RUET

The AI-Powered Interactive Robot is a voice-controlled intelligent assistant that integrates artificial intelligence with embedded systems. The project demonstrates real-time speech recognition, emotion-based interaction, and IoT device control. The system achieved a Top 5 position at the university project fair for its innovative design and practical engineering implementation.
